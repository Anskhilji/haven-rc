Single Family Rentals

In 2010, Haven Realty Capital recognized an opportunity to acquire, stabilize, lease and manage distressed residential properties in working class neighborhoods. The strategy has provided a valid financial opportunity due to strong yield metrics and realized value appreciation. Equally as important, execution of the strategy aided in the stabilization of local neighborhoods and provided a viable and effective solution to the U.S. housing crisis. Since inception, Haven Realty Capital has acquired, renovated and stabilized 1,500+ single family rental homes as a principal owner and has third party managed approximately 10,000 single family rental homes across seven states.

Bella View in Snellville

Bella View is a new 91-home community with newly built, for-rent single family homes located in Snellville, GA, approximately 25 miles northeast of Downtown Atlanta. Each of the four- and five-bedroom homes feature modern finishes, premier kitchens, two-car garages, large yards and pet-friendly environments.

The Reserve in Dallas

The Reserve is an 86-single family rental home development located on the backside of a larger community known as The Reserve at Timberlands in Dallas, GA. The Reserve offers spacious three- and four-bedroom floorplans and each home features with modern finishes, premier kitchens, home office capability, master-on-the-main options, two-car garages, and large yards with maintenance-free packages.

MacLand Township in Dallas

MacLand Township is a popular neighborhood of newly built, for-rent homes in Dallas, GA, 40 miles northwest of Downtown Atlanta. The 155-home community features both three- and four-bedroom floorplans and six elevations. Homes ranging in size from 1,649 square feet to 2,335 square feet. Dallas, GA is located in Paulding County, which was named among the “20 Best Places to Live” in the United States by CNN.

Trickum Hills in Lawrenceville

Trickum Hill is a dedicated rental community located in Lawrenceville, GA, one of Atlanta’s most sought-out suburbs. Trickum Hill features 78 newly built townhomes with three- and four-bedroom floorplans built around a central park with an amenity pavilion.

Moss Pointe in Canton

Moss Pointe is a community of 49 newly built homes located in Canton, GA 35 miles north of Downtown Atlanta. The four-bedroom, two-and-half bathroom homes range in size from 2,200 - 2,361 square feet.

Magnolia Trace in Holly Springs

Magnolia Trace is a dedicated rental community tucked into the forest-blanketed suburb of Holly Springs, GA 35 miles north of Downtown Georgia. The 78 four-bedroom, two-and-a-half bathroom floorplans average approximately 2,300 square feet.

Ashwood Place

Ashwood Place is a newly constructed 60-home dedicated rental community in the Chicago suburb of Naperville, IL and one of the first DRCs in Chicagoland area. Each of the brick and vinyl clad single-story residences at Ashwood Place feature a two-bedroom, two-bath floorplan with 1,350 square feet of living space. Interior amenities include central air, cathedral ceilings, granite countertops, stainless steel energy efficient appliances, washer and dryer, wood style flooring and a two-car attached garage.

Harrison Landing Townes in Simpsonville

Harrison Landing Townes is being developed by a national homebuilder on an 18-acre site at 106 McGuires Place in Simpsonville, SC 21 miles SE of downtown Greenville. Each of the two-story, 1,564-square-foot townhomes feature three-bedrooms, two-and-a-half baths, attached garage, luxury vinyl plank floors, stainless-steel appliances, granite countertops, tile backsplash and nine-foot ceilings on the first floor. Community amenities include a dog park, playground and walking trail.
